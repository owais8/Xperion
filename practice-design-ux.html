Why Us

The new bar isn't "looks nice". It's earns trust.

Users now decide in seconds whether your product is credible, and AI-generated noise has raised the cost of looking generic. Trust comes from research-led decisions, accessible execution, and the kind of detail that survives a real workflow, not a demo gif.

Generative tooling is great at variation. It's not great at deciding which variation is the right one. We don't outsource that decision.
  • ◇
    Senior designers onlyMinimum 7+ years and a portfolio of shipped work. We don't pair junior designers with AI tools and call it leverage.
  • ◐
    Research-ledGenerative interviews, usability testing, and synthesis turn assumptions into evidence before you commit roadmap or capital.
  • ⊞

Process

Four phases. Research → ship.

Same operating cadence as the rest of Xperion. Research before pixels. Tokens before screens. Tests before launch.

01
Phase 01 · 1-2 wks

Discovery

Stakeholder alignment, audit, research plan, success-metric definition, costed proposal.

02
Phase 02 · 2-3 wks

Definition

Research synthesis, design principles, token architecture, signed direction at close.

03
Phase 03 · Ongoing

Delivery

Sprint cadence with weekly demos. Engineering handoff is part of the design, not an afterthought.

04
Phase 04 · Ongoing

Optimise

Post-launch usability testing, optimisation, and the design-system stewardship that keeps the work cohesive over time.
