Challenge

Financial AI doesn't fail because the model is wrong. It fails because the governance isn't there.

The model that scores well on a holdout set doesn't ship without model risk documentation, bias evaluation, ongoing monitoring, fallback logic, and a clear human-in-the-loop story. Xperion's financial-services engagements build the governance, evidence, and operational rigour alongside the system, not after.

An AI feature in a bank is a regulated change. We've staffed both sides of that conversation, first line and second line.
  • ▲
    Model risk-aware engineeringSR 11-7 / OCC 2011-12 framing, model documentation, validation evidence, and ongoing performance monitoring, designed in, not retrofitted.
  • ✓
    Audit-grade traceabilityDecision logs, prompt & response capture, model versions, and explainability artefacts captured at production speed. Subpoena-ready.
  • ⊕
    Compliance-by-designBSA/AML, KYC/CIP, OFAC screening, FCRA, GLBA, ECOA, UDAP, wired into the build, not bolted on at the end.
  • ◆
    Second-line collaborationWe work with your model risk, compliance, and audit teams from week one. The evidence pack is a deliverable, not a surprise.
