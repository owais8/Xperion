Challenge

The shop floor doesn't care about your cloud diagram.

Most industrial AI projects fail at the OT/IT seam, the model trains on clean data extracts but doesn't survive the real PLC, MES, ERP, WMS, or TMS environment it has to live inside. Xperion's industrial engagements start from the asset, the process, and the integration constraint backwards.

If the operator can't trust it during a bad shift, it doesn't matter how well it scored in the notebook.
  • ⊞
    OT/IT integration-fluentNative fluency with MES, ERP, WMS, TMS, SCADA, historians (PI, Ignition), and the OPC UA / MQTT layer between them.
  • ⊕
    Edge-to-cloud architectureDesigns that run inference at the line where latency or air-gap matters, and in cloud where economies and observability matter.
  • ▲
    Procurement-aware deliveryCapex framing, TCO modelling, vendor-management language. We speak the language of operations, supply-chain leadership, and IT procurement.
  • ◆
    Operator-first UXTools designed for gloves, glare, low bandwidth, and shift changes, not the conference-room demo.
