The Screen

The vetting screen, stage by stage.

Most firms say “pre-vetted” and stop there. This is the screen every Xperion specialist passes before a client conversation, and what you receive from it.

01
Stage 01 · Craft

Craft depth

A working session with a senior practitioner from the same discipline: engineers screen engineers, designers screen designers. We probe systems the candidate has shipped, decisions they got wrong, and what they’d change now. Production scars over tool names.

02
Stage 02 · Problem

Working problem

A real problem from a past Xperion engagement, anonymised, worked live. We watch how the candidate reasons through unfamiliar constraints, not whether they recall a textbook answer.

03
Stage 03 · Communication

Written and spoken

A written brief and a live walkthrough in English, the way distributed senior work actually happens. Strong builders who can’t write clearly, or won’t push back constructively, stop here.

04
Stage 04 · Fit

Fit & references

Working-style review against your specific context, including stage, cadence, and leadership style, plus reference conversations with people who shipped alongside the candidate. Embedding is the product; solo brilliance isn’t enough.

Every shortlisted candidate arrives with the screen’s output: a dossier covering all four stages, a work-sample summary, reference notes, and the engagement manager’s match rationale. Ask us to walk through one on the scoping call.
