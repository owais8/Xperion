Challenge

Healthcare AI lives or dies at the intersection of workflow, compliance, and integration.

Most health-tech failures aren't algorithmic, they're operational. The model performs in the lab but breaks against EHR integration realities. The patient-facing app launches without accessibility. Xperion's healthcare engagements start from the clinical workflow and the compliance burden backwards.

The model that works in the notebook is not the system that works in the EHR. We engineer for the second one.
  • ✓
    HIPAA-aligned by defaultBAA-eligible cloud, encryption at rest and in transit, minimum-necessary access, and audit trails wired in from day one.
  • ⊞
    EHR-aware engineeringNative fluency with Epic, Cerner / Oracle Health, Athena, and FHIR R4, we know where the joins fail before we write them.
  • ◆
    Clinician-in-the-loop designWorkflow shadowing, time-motion analysis, and clinician advisors on every major engagement. AI that adds seconds dies fast.
  • ▲
    Evidence-first deliveryValidation packs, audit logs, model cards, and bias evaluations shipped alongside the code, not retrofitted under deadline.
