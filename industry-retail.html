Challenge

The bar isn't traffic. It's relevance, speed, and the post-purchase loop.

Retail technology has split between commerce platforms that promise the moon and growth teams shipping a hundred experiments a quarter. The brands that compound have a different shape: a tight stack, a clear point of view, and AI in the workflow where it sharpens, not where it generates noise.

The win isn't more traffic. It's more revenue per session and the second visit that follows.
  • ◐
    Conversion-economics fluentDesigned around AOV, CVR, contribution margin, repeat rate, and CAC payback, not impressions or sessions in isolation.
  • ⊞
    Composable, performance-firstHeadless and composable architectures where they pay back, monolith where they don't. Core Web Vitals as a delivery constraint, not an afterthought.
  • ◈
    AI in the workflowLLM-augmented merchandising, content production, support, and lifecycle, quietly faster, never sloppier, and instrumented like any other revenue surface.
  • ⊕
    Full-funnel attributionWarehouse-native attribution and MMM-friendly tagging set up before the first campaign ships. Every dollar measurable.
