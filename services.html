Why one roof

Most companies juggle four vendors to ship one product.

The cost of fragmentation isn't just procurement. It's the context lost between strategy and build, the design language that drifts between product and marketing, and the talent that arrives without the domain. We built Xperion to compound across all of it.

Talent and technology aren't separate offerings here. They're one compounding loop, and brand, strategy, and design wrap around them.

Engagement models

Three ways to start. Combine as you grow.

Every practice supports the same three engagement shapes, pick whichever fits the moment and the budget cadence you actually have.

Model 01 · Sprint

Focused sprint

A fixed-scope, fixed-price sprint to answer one question, AI pilot, strategy sprint, brand sprint, design sprint, or focused build.

  • Fixed price & scope
  • 2-6 week duration
  • Signed deliverable
  • Optional follow-on
Most Popular
Model 02 · Embedded

Embedded specialist or pod

A senior specialist or multi-disciplinary pod embedded into your team, under your direction, on your tools, sprint-aligned.

  • Senior, domain-matched
  • 1-2 week placement
  • Monthly retainer cadence
  • Engagement manager support
Model 03 · Programme

Multi-quarter programme

A larger transformation or build programme with sequenced phases, named governance, and direct handoff into delivery.

  • Quarterly governance
  • Risk & dependency tracking
  • Cross-practice teams
  • Quarterly business reviews
