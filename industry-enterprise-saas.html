Challenge

"Add an AI feature" is not the same as build AI that earns NRR.

The market is flooded with SaaS products that bolted on a chat widget and called it AI-native. The features that compound ARR are different, they sit deep in workflow, they earn trust through reliability, and they're instrumented like the rest of the product.

The AI feature your customers love is the one they use on Tuesday morning, not the one they screenshotted at launch.
